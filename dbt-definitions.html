DBT Skills Definitions

Mindfulness – Observe, Describe, Participate in the moment without judgment.

Distress Tolerance – TIP (Temperature, Intense exercise, Paced breathing, Progressive relaxation), ACCEPTS (Activities, Contributing, Comparisons, Emotions, Pushing away, Thoughts, Sensations), and Self-Soothe (use the five senses to calm yourself).

Emotion Regulation – Check the Facts (do your emotions fit the facts?), Opposite Action (act opposite to unhelpful emotions).

Interpersonal Effectiveness – DEAR MAN (assert needs), GIVE (relationship skills), FAST (self-respect effectiveness).
