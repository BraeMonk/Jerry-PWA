DBT Skills Definitions

TIP – Change your body chemistry quickly: Temperature, Intense exercise, Paced breathing, Progressive relaxation.
ACCEPTS – Distract with: Activities, Contributing, Comparisons, Emotions, Pushing away, Thoughts, Sensations.
Self-Soothe – Use your five senses (sight, sound, touch, taste, smell) to comfort yourself.
Check the Facts – Look at whether your feelings match the facts of the situation.
Opposite Action – Do the opposite of what your emotion is telling you, if the emotion doesn’t fit the facts.
DEAR MAN – Be assertive: Describe, Express, Assert, Reinforce, stay Mindful, Appear confident, Negotiate.
GIVE – Build relationships: Gentle, Interested, Validate, Easy manner.
FAST – Maintain self-respect: Fair, no Apologies for existing, Stick to values, Truthful.
